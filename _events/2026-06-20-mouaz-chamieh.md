Are you actually moving your data, or just asking the compiler nicely?

Move semantics have been in C++ for over a decade, but the industry is still littered with misconceptions, undefined behaviors, and missed optimizations. Don't let deep copies destroy your performance. Join Mouaz Chamieh at GlobalCPP virtual presentation for a dive into the real mechanics of C++ move semantics. We'll debug the myths, map out the pitfalls, and show you how to leverage true zero-copy efficiency

**About the Presenter**

Mouaz Chamieh is a software architect with over 12 years of professional experience. He holds academic degrees in computer science and has a strong background in C++, software architecture, and adherence to industry coding standards. He is passionate about patterns and best practices which deliver clean designed/coded, testable, maintainable, scalable, secure, and high-performance software.
