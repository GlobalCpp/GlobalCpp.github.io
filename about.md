**Global C++** is a collaboration between numerous regional C++ user groups. Our
goal is simple: bring the world's local C++ communities together, share great
technical talks, and make it easy for anyone to learn and connect — wherever they
are. It is a volunteer-run, community-first effort, and we're always glad to have
more people join us.

## Weekly online sessions

Every Saturday we host a free online tech presentation from a speaker in the
wider C++ community — language features, performance, tooling, and war stories
from real systems. Sessions run live on Zoom, are usually streamed to
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@GlobalCpp){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"},
and recordings are posted afterwards (pending volunteer effort). Questions are
welcome live or in the [Discord](https://discord.gg/HVv7Jya37T){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}
if you'd rather not appear on stream.

Browse everything we've hosted on the [Events](/events/) page, and never miss a
talk by subscribing to the
[Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0?cid=NDdjMjI1ZTAyYTFkNjdkNWNmZjVhY2EzMDk1YjMzMWEyODRlZDQ4ZTQ4YTlkZDAxMTYyODJhYjEzZGM0MmQ3MEBncm91cC5jYWxlbmRhci5nb29nbGUuY29t){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}
or the [iCal feed](/events.ics) (works with Apple Calendar, Outlook, and any
calendar app — paste the URL as a subscribed calendar).

## Join a local group

Global C++ is made up of independent regional user groups around the world. Find
one near you on the [Member Groups](/members/) page and its interactive map.

## Bring your group in

If you run a C++ user group and would like to collaborate — share talks, cross-post
events, and appear on the map — reach out on the
[Global C++ Discord](https://discord.gg/HVv7Jya37T){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.
We're very much a growing effort and would love to have you.
